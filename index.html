Visit Ann Arbor

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant college town, home of the University of Michigan.

Top three activities to do in Ann Arbor

Attend a concert a show or concert

The Ark is a great little music venue specializing in American folk music.

Get a Reuben sandwich at Zingerman's Deli

Zingerman's Deli is a local staple and a place you must try on your vist.

Go see a game at the largest stadium in America

Michigan Stadium is known as the Big House, it seats 107,501 people, it's an experience like no other.
